PRISON ESCAPE GAME PLANNING


Items found in prison:
    -Spoon: Can be found in the kitchen
    -Toothbrush: Can be found in the bathroom
    -Piece of Metal: Can be found in the workshop
    -Rope: Can be found in the storage room
    -Food: Can be found in cafeteria

Items you can only get from helping prisoners:
    -Screwdriver: Can get from cellmate, if you give him money, you get money from doing a shift in the kitchen.
    -Piece of Metal: Can get from prisoner in the workshop, he left his toothbrush in the bathroom and asks you to get it.
    -Fireworks: Can get from prisoner in bathroom, if you give him food.
    -If I need to get another item off a person I will make them be in the cafeteria.
        
Items you can make:
    -Makeshift Weapon: Made by combining toothbrush and piece of metal in the workshop.
    -Lockpick: Can be made by combining a piece of metal and a screwdriver.



Escape Route 1: Guard Disguise
    Steps:
    1. Find toothbrush
    2. Find piece of metal
    3. Make makeshift weapon
    4. Use makeshift weapon to incapacitate guard
    5. Take guard's uniform
    6. Steal guard's keycard
    7. Walk out front door

Escape Route 2. Vent System
    Steps:
    1. Find screwdriver
    2. Open vent in bathroom
    3. Crawl through vent system
    4. Reach guard office
    5. Steal guard's keycard
    6. Walk out front door

Escape Route 3. Wall Climb
    Steps:
    1. Find Rope
    2. Find metal
    3. Make makeshift grappling hook
    4. Distract gaurds with Fireworks
    5. Climb out

Escape routes can be randomly unavaliable
Guard Disguise - Guard fights back and wins
Vent System - Vents are blocked off
Wall climb - Rope is too short
